 The Arthrix Push Lock is a knotless suture anchor designed for quick and secure shoulder instability repair. The Push Lock utilizes a suture first technique that allows the surgeon to capture the proper amount of labrum and capsule prior to anchor insertion. The fiber wires are delivered into the bone socket. is clearly visualized, adjusted, and then locked into position with the push lock anchor body creating a strong and low profile construct without knots. The suture first technique also allows the freedom for multiple stitch configurations like cinch stitch and mattress stitches. The knotless push lock saves steps and valuable time while providing a secure repair that and eliminates issues with knot impingement.
